Learning the language

Now you've acquainted yourself with the Nulus and the Gilis, you're ready to start learning their language.

On each screen, you will see:

  • an alien word
  • an image of the object or objects that the word refers to
  • an alien saying the word

Your task is to try to learn what the alien words are for different objects and how the language works.

Click 'Next' button when you are ready.
